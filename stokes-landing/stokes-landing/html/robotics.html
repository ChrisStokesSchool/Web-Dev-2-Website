The Game:

The game being played for the current V5RC season is called "High Stakes" and it revolves around stacking rings onto several different kinds of posts called "Stakes". At the beginning of every match you are paired with another random robot and given a color. That color will determin what color rings you are trying to score. A ring has to be encircling a stake to be considered scored and a ring on the top of a scored ring stack is worth three points. Every ring that is not a top ring but is still considered scored is only worth one point. There are also modifiers in each corner of the feild that will either double the point values of every ring on a stake inside of the corner or convert the scored rings' points into negative points that will take points away from you at the end of the match. This is a very bare bones summary that leaves out a bunch so if you want to learn more you can read the official rulebook.
