Products I Enjoy Using


As I have gotten older I have started to appreciate the value of good quality products at a relativly low price. Because of this, I feel like providing a list of budget products on this website is appropriate. Here are some products (not nessesarily brands) that I feel comfortable endorsing.

Product Image

Fifine K678:

Price: $59.99

Link: https://www.amazon.com/FIFINE-Microphone-Recording-PS4-Headphone-K678/dp/B085WRN1VD


This is the microphone that I have at my desk at all times. For the price, it sounds amazing and it is definitely better than any built in mic you are going to find. It also does go on sale occasionally. The only big downside is that it uses Mini USB and I know that can be a deal breaker for some people.

CamelBak Chute Mag 20oz Waterbottle

Price: $15.00

Link: https://www.amazon.com/CamelBak-Chute-Bottle-Tritan-Charcoal/dp/B08QVC2ZWH?ref_=ast_sto_dp


This is the water bottle that I daily drive. It is a plastic water bottle but it is by far the best plastic water bottle I have ever used. It comes in a variety of colors and it has a lid that uses magnets to help seal off the spout. It is also dishwasher safe.

PopSocket Phone Grip

Price: $9.97

Link: https://www.amazon.com/PopSockets-PopGrip-Swappable-Phones-Tablets/dp/B07P29XQR4


At this point most people know what a pop socket is but I don't see a ton of people using them. I think that that is a shame because I absolutly love mine. It is super comfortable to one hand my phone using the popsocket and it doubles as a kick stand. You can also get PopSockets with pictures on them if you like that sort of thing.

Soundance Adjustable Laptop Stand

Price: $25.99

Link: https://www.amazon.com/Soundance-Adjustable-Computer-Ergonomic-Compatible/dp/...


If you use a laptop instead of a PC then you know the pain trying to look at a laptop's screen can cause you. For most people, myself included, laptop screens are just to low to the ground to be comfortable to use. A laptop stand fixes this because it allows you to position the laptop at the spot that is the most ergonomic for you. This stand is the one I use but you should be sure to get one that fits your laptop.

SABRENT 4-Port USB 3.0 Hub

Price: $19.99

Link: https://www.amazon.com/Sabrent-4-Port-Individual-Switches-HB-UM43/dp/B00JX1ZS5O/ref=zg_bs_g_17387627011_d_sccl_19/131-8605848-6011107?th=1


Accessories are a big part of using a computer and 90% of accessories require a USB port to use. There have been many times that I just simply did not have enough USB ports to use everything I needed to use. This usb hub helps to solve that problem by splitting 1 USB port into multiple. Some other features that this hub has are off switches for all of its usb ports and USB 3.0 compatibility.
